    Creamy Chicken Sauce with Steamed Rice

    A rich, creamy chicken sauce packed with tender chunks of chicken, sautéed onions, bell peppers, and herbs — served alongside a perfectly molded portion of steamed white rice. Garnished with crispy fried onions, cucumber, lettuce, and a fresh tomato slice for a refreshing touch.

    A satisfying dish that’s full of flavor, comfort, and elegance.

    Citrus & Berry Fruit Salad

    A refreshing medley of thinly sliced oranges, grapefruits, and pineapples — beautifully layered and topped with juicy blueberries, pomegranate seeds, crunchy pistachios, and fresh mint leaves.

    Bursting with color, flavor, and nutrients, this salad is the perfect light and healthy treat for any time of day.

    Spicy Noodle Soup Bowl

    A hearty and aromatic bowl of noodles swimming in a rich, spiced broth. Packed with tender meat chunks, soft tofu slices, fresh herbs, bean sprouts, and leafy greens — this dish delivers bold flavor, warmth, and comfort in every spoonful.

    Perfect for spice lovers and noodle fans alike!

    Mediterranean Hummus Platter

    A beautifully arranged platter of creamy hummus, topped with fresh parsley, crumbled feta, and juicy cherry tomatoes. At the center, a stack of crispy pita chips adds the perfect crunch to every bite. Garnished with sweet red pepper ribbons for a festive finish — this dish is as eye-catching as it is delicious.

    Ideal as a starter, party dip, or a light, healthy snack to share.

    Superfood Fruit & Spinach Bowl

    A refreshing and nutrient-packed bowl featuring layers of baby spinach, sweet bananas, juicy mango chunks, antioxidant-rich blueberries, and a sprinkle of chia seeds for added fiber and texture. Lightly dusted with cinnamon and flaxseed for a flavorful, health-boosting finish.

    Perfect as a power breakfast, post-workout snack, or a light vegan meal.
